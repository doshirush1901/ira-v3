ALP Group uses our custom PF1-3520 to produce Mahindra Scorpio pickup truck bedliners in 5.5mm HDPE — featuring an innovative sag-following heater system designed from scratch. 🚀

Here's how we made it happen:

- **3500 x 2000 mm forming area** — one of the largest single-station machines for bedliner production.
- **Sag-following bottom heater** — tracks HDPE sheet sag in real-time using sensors, ensuring consistent heating and top-quality output.
- **560 IR ceramic heating elements** with 8-zone PLC control for precise temperature management.
- **Automatic sheet loading** and **closed-loop cooling** for increased production efficiency.

What it means: Our innovative design eliminates guesswork and ensures optimal heating, resulting in high-quality bedliners for Mahindra. 

This project was a full turnkey discussion — from machine supply to trimming and tooling. We partnered with ALP Group, a $200M automotive components leader, to make their entry into the pickup truck bedliner market seamless. The machine is up and running at their Nashik plant, and we’re proud to be the sole supplier for Mahindra's bedliner component.

Our collaboration doesn’t stop at machine supply; we’re deepening our partnership by providing consulting services and introducing new technologies. This project exemplifies our commitment to being a long-term thermoforming partner, not just a vendor.
