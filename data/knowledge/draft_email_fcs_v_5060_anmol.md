With reference to your requirement, I have put the offer and key specs below so you have everything in one place. We are offering the **FCS-V-5060** — a 3-station inline machine with **vacuum forming only** (no air pressure). Heating is by **IR ceramic heaters**, top and bottom, zone-controlled via PLC for even heating and consistent cycles. The **spike chain** that carries the sheet through the three stations is **servo-indexed**, so you get accurate positioning station to station and repeatable indexing every cycle. At the forming station the main movements are **pneumatic**; at the punching station the tool is **servo motor driven** for precise cut and trim. Max platen size **500 × 650 mm**. Same FCS family we have supplied for packaging and formed parts where vacuum-only is sufficient.
