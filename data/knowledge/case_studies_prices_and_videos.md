# Case studies — prices and videos for lead emails

Use when drafting to leads (e.g. Plastikabalumag, European thermoformers) to reference specific machines, case studies, and proof points. **Check case studies** (e.g. in `11_Project_Case_Studies` or `data/case_studies/`) when suggesting machines that could be interesting for them.

---

## PF1-0707-LAB (Lab machine)

- **Case study PDF:** `Machinecraft_Case_Study_PF1-0707-LAB.pdf` (e.g. in 11_Project_Case_Studies)
- **Price:** **110K EUR**
- **Video:** https://youtu.be/dbx5ZeU4wWs
- **Use for:** R&D, prototyping, small-batch, lab environments, quick changeover (universal frames). Good for European thermoformers asking about a compact/lab machine.

---

## IMG / IAC (In-Mold Graining — automotive soft-touch)

- **Case study PDF:** `IMGS -1350_ Machinecraft IAC Project Summary.pdf` (e.g. in 11_Project_Case_Studies)
- **Price:** **250K EUR** (reference: IAC project order value in INR; for international/EUR use 250K EUR as stated)
- **Application:** Applying **soft-touch artificial leather / thermoplastic skin over hard plastic substrate** (instrument panels, door trims, center consoles, armrests). India’s first made-in-India IMG thermoforming machine.
- **Videos:**  
  - https://youtu.be/ZumfV-uoCaY  
  - https://www.machinecraft.org/news/img-thermoforming-automotive
- **Use for:** Automotive interiors, Tier-1 suppliers, soft-touch surfaces. Good for leads in **automotive** or **surface technology**.

---

## Dezet Story (PF1-X)

- **Case study PDF:** `Dezet Story.pdf` (e.g. in 11_Project_Case_Studies)
- **Machine:** **PF1-X-1310**
- **Price:** **150K EUR**
- **Video:** https://youtu.be/g_KmBeIqFes
- **Context:** Dezet (Netherlands) — precision thermoforming for mobility, aerospace, medical; replaced 42-year-old Illig. PF1-X-1210 in case_study_data (1200×1000 mm); for lead emails use **PF1-X-1310 at 150K EUR** per user.
- **Use for:** European thermoformers, aerospace/medical/mobility, precision parts, replacement of old equipment.

---

## ALP Bedliner (PF1-3520)

- **Application:** HDPE pickup truck bedliners; 3500×2000 mm, deep-draw, sag-following heater. Custom project.
- **Video:** https://youtu.be/ErfTIYoZLyg
- **Use for:** Heavy-gauge, automotive/truck, off-highway.

---

## Dutch Tides XL (PF1-X-6520)

- **Application:** Largest thermoformer in Europe; 6.5×2 m forming area; hydroponic ebb-flow trays (Dutch Tides, Netherlands). Custom XL.
- **Video:** https://youtu.be/8pnNCwBbySE
- **Use for:** Very large format, agriculture/hydroponics.

---

## Formpack sheets (cross-sell)

- **Product:** ABS/ASA with UV & FR, max width 2.5 m; new extrusion line. ~50 tonnes ABS/month.
- **Video:** https://youtu.be/vuhSxOKlm_w
- **Use for:** Sheet supply cross-sell when discussing machines with leads who use ABS/ASA.
