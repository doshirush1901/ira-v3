## Learned from 20 Real Email Exchanges (Feb 2026)

*Source: Single Station Inquiry Form leads with actual email conversations*

## Customer Inquiry Patterns

### 1. Investment Planning Timeline
Customers often mention government funding/investment plans:
```
"Today we started officially the investment plan. To be able to realize it 
we need now to wait for the government. This can take some months."
```
**IRA should:** Acknowledge timeline, offer to stay in touch, be patient.

### 2. Technical Clarification Requests
Customers ask for clarification on roll feeders, sheet loading:
```
"We have seen your videos, would be interested in roll feeder.
Can you get your team in Gurgaon connect up with me through mail."
```
**IRA should:** Offer to connect local team, ask clarifying questions.

### 3. Distributor/Partnership Discussions
Some leads are potential distributors asking about:
- OEM pricing/discounts
- Stock machines for showrooms
- NPE show demos
```
"Is there an OEM discount? Should we mark this up to our customer? 
Would our customer get quoted less if they go direct to you?"
```
**IRA should:** Explain partnership model, offer 5% OEM discount for strategic partners.

## Rushabh's Response Patterns

### Pattern 1: Comprehensive Quotation Email
For serious inquiries, Rushabh sends detailed quotes:
```
Machine Configuration Summary
Based on your detailed specifications, we are pleased to offer:

Machine Model: PF1-X-2615
Investment: EUR 290,000 (EXW Umbergaum, Gujarat, India)
Quotation No.: MT20251223
Valid Until: January 16, 2026

Key Specifications:
• Maximum forming area: 2600 × 1500 mm
• Minimum forming area: 1600 × 750 mm
• Maximum depth: 800 mm
• Sheet thickness range: 2-10 mm
...
```

### Pattern 2: Clarifying Questions
Before quoting, Rushabh asks:
```
"Thanks for filling out the RFQ sheet. We are preparing techno-commercial
offer for your review, could you clarify the following:
1, Would you like the sheet loading to be done manually or with a roll feeder?
2, What is your budget range?
..."
```

### Pattern 3: Multi-Option Proposals
For shows/demos, Rushabh offers multiple options:
```
"Regarding the machine that is possible at the expo, please see the 2 x 
possible options due to booth size restriction:

1, Video link of similar machine: [URL]
   - max. forming area 3 x 6 feet
   - sell price: approx. 85,000 USD, EXW

2, Video link of similar machine: [URL]
   - max. forming area 2.6 x 3.2 feet
   - sell price: approx. 85,000 USD, EXW
"
```

### Pattern 4: Deadline Setting
```
"It takes us 4 months to produce both these machines, so a decision must 
come by first week of Jan 2024!"
```
