# CAD / Tool Design Engineer — role requirements (Machinecraft Umargam)

**Source:** Derived from *Recruitment Strategy for Senior CAD_Tool Design Engineer (Automotive, Mumbai)* and Machinecraft production process. Use for tailoring Stage 2 emails to CAD candidates.

---

## What we need

- **Design and drawing release** for thermoforming machinery (PF1, PF2, IMG, FCS, AM series): fabrication drawings, weldment packs, base plates, frames. Drawings must be released in line with BOM freeze so fabrication can start on time.
- **BOM and sequencing:** Ability to work with BOM freeze, task codes (O:, R:, W:, FT:), and coordination with Procurement and Factory Head. Understanding of when a drawing block affects dispatch.
- **Software:** Proficiency in at least one of: SolidWorks, AutoCAD, NX, CATIA, Creo, or equivalent for 2D/3D design and detailing. Experience with GD&T and machine-building or sheet-metal fabrication is a plus.
- **Collaboration:** Clear communication with Production, Procurement, and CAD lead; ability to handle revision requests and last-minute changes without blocking the shop floor unnecessarily.
- **Location:** Role is at **Umargam**. Willingness to relocate or commute is essential.

---

## How the role fits

CAD sits between design intent and fabrication: drawings are released in packs, fabrication cannot start a phase without released drawings, and delays in release directly impact dispatch. We look for candidates who can own drawing release for a project, coordinate with the Factory Head on revisions, and keep the production flow unblocked.
