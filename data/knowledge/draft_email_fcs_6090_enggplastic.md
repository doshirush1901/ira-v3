**Tech specs (FCS 6090)**  
• Machine type: Inline pressure forming, 3 stations (heat → form → unload)  
• Forming area: 600 × 900 mm per station  
• Process: Vacuum + pressure forming — crisp detail, food packaging, thicker-gauge parts  
• Heating: IR ceramic — top and bottom banks, zone-controlled via PLC  
• Movements: Electric servo — indexing, forming stroke, tool clamp  
• Vacuum / pressure: Dedicated system per station (exact pump/spec on formal quote)  
• Control: PLC + HMI touchscreen (Mitsubishi)  
• Sheet: Roll feeding using Air Cylinder Based Roll Stand
