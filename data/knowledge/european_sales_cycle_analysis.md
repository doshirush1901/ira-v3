## Sales Stage Flow Analysis

### Typical Winning Flow
```
Inquiry → Technical Discussion → Quote → Factory Visit → Negotiation → PO
```

### Stage Breakdown

**1. Inquiry Stage (Days 1-7)**
- Initial contact via email, website, or trade show
- Customer describes application and requirements
- Rushabh response within 24 hours with preliminary info

**2. Technical Discussion (Days 7-30)**
- Detailed specification exchange
- Machine sizing based on part dimensions
- Material compatibility confirmation
- 3-5 technical emails typically

**3. Quote Stage (Days 14-60)**
- Formal quotation with all specs
- Options and configurations explained
- Video links to similar machines in operation

**4. Factory Visit Invitation (Days 30-90)**
- Key differentiator for Machinecraft
- Offer to visit European reference site (DutchTides, Dezet)
- Or invitation to India factory
- Converts ~40% of visitors to buyers

**5. Negotiation (Days 60-180)**
- Price discussion
- Payment terms (typically 30-40-30)
- Delivery timeline
- Training and installation scope

**6. Closing/PO (Variable)**
- Purchase order received
- Proforma invoice issued
- Production begins

## Winning Techniques by Duration

### For Fast Closers (<3 months)
- Immediate video call to build rapport
- Same-day quote turnaround
- Factory visit offer within first week
- Reference customer in same country
- Urgency: "production slot available"

### For Medium Cycles (3-12 months)
- Monthly check-in emails
- Share relevant case studies
- Trade show meeting invitations
- Technical webinars/demonstrations
- Patient persistence without pressure

### For Long Nurture (>12 months)
- Quarterly newsletters
- Industry news sharing
- Price protection offers
- Relationship building over transactions
- "When you're ready, we're here" approach

## Country-Specific Insights

### Sweden (4 customers - €360,000)
- Longest average cycle (~35 months)
- Relationship-driven market
- Quality over price focus
- Strong repeat business potential

### Germany (2 customers - €450,000)
- Technical evaluation critical
- Competitive with local manufacturers (CMS, Illig)
- Value proposition: Indian efficiency + European quality
- Medium cycle (~2 months average)

### Netherlands (2 customers - €800,000)
- Highest value market per customer
- Strong reference site value (DutchTides)
- Innovative applications (hydroponics)
- Batelaan now closed (2026)

### UK & Ireland (2 customers - €190,000)
- Fastest closers
- Direct decision-making
- Price competitive market
- Good reference potential

## For Ira: Sales Cycle Guidance

When a new European inquiry comes in:

1. **Estimate Cycle Length**
   - Startup/new company: 12-24 months (funding dependent)
   - Established, clear need: 1-3 months
   - Replacing old machine: 1-6 months
   - Expansion/new line: 6-12 months

2. **Set Email Cadence**
   - Hot lead: 2-3 touches per week
   - Warm lead: Weekly
   - Nurture: Monthly

3. **Key Conversion Points**
   - After quote: Offer factory visit
   - After 30 days silence: Share relevant case study
   - After 90 days: Check if requirements changed
   - After 6 months: Ask about timeline update

4. **Deal Size Expectations**
   - Standard PF1: €60,000-€100,000
   - Large format PF1-X: €150,000-€300,000
   - Extra-large PF1-X-65XX: €500,000+
