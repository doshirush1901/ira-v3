# Mirsant — Bathtub Manufacturing, Russia

## Customer
- **Name:** Mirsant
- **Location:** Russia
- **Industry:** Sanitary ware / Bathtub manufacturing

## Project Overview
Mirsant needed a high-throughput bathtub manufacturing line capable of producing
25 bathtubs per hour. Bathtub thermoforming is one of the most demanding applications —
deep draw (860mm), thick acrylic/ABS sheets, and the need for perfect surface finish
with no marks, scratches, or optical defects visible to end consumers.

## Machine
- **Model:** PF1-2116
- **Forming Area:** 2100 x 1600 mm
- **Max Depth:** 860 mm (deep draw for full-size bathtubs)
- **Order Value:** USD 240,000
- **Year:** 2022

## Technical Specifications
- **Heater Load:** 288 kW total
- **Connected Load:** 330 kW
- **Heater Type:** IR Halogen (fastest heating, most uniform for thick sheets)
- **Vacuum:** 200 m³/hr
- **Autoloader:** Yes (automatic sheet loading for continuous production)
- **Universal Frames:** Temperature-controlled (prevents edge cooling/warping)
- **Production Rate:** 25 bathtubs per hour
- **Deep Draw:** 860mm depth — one of the deepest forming depths in Machinecraft's range

## Story Angle
A bathtub-a-minute machine. Mirsant's PF1-2116 produces 25 bathtubs per hour with
IR halogen heaters for the fastest, most uniform heating. The 860mm draw depth handles
full-size bathtubs, and temperature-controlled universal frames prevent the edge defects
that plague cheaper machines. This is industrial-scale sanitary ware production.
