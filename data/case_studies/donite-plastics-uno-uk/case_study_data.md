# Donite Plastics — e-UNO Compact Thermoformer, UK

## Customer
- **Name:** Donite Plastics Ltd
- **Location:** United Kingdom
- **Industry:** General thermoforming / Plastics processing

## Project Overview
Donite Plastics needed a compact, versatile thermoforming machine that could fit in
a small factory footprint while still delivering precise, repeatable results. The
e-UNO-8060 is Machinecraft's answer to the compact machine market — fully electric,
zone-controlled heating, and small enough to ship in a single 20ft container.

## Machine
- **Model:** e-UNO-8060
- **Type:** UNO (compact single-station)
- **Forming Area:** 800 x 600 mm
- **Max Depth:** 400 mm
- **Order Value:** EUR 87,000
- **Year:** 2022

## Technical Specifications
- **Heater Load:** 22.4 kW total
- **Connected Load:** 34 kW (low energy consumption)
- **Heater Type:** IR Quartz, zone controlled (individual zone temperature control)
- **Vacuum:** 40 m³/hr
- **All Movements:** Electric Servo (fully electric, no pneumatics/hydraulics)
- **Footprint:** 1.9 x 2.7 x 3.2 m (compact)
- **Shipping:** Fits in 1 x 20ft container (easy global logistics)

## Story Angle
Not every customer needs a 6-meter monster. The e-UNO proves Machinecraft can build
precision compact machines too. Fully electric, zone-controlled, and small enough to
ship in a standard container — this is the entry-level machine that opens doors to
new markets and smaller processors who need quality without the massive footprint.
