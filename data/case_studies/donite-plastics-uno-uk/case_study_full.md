## The Challenge

Donite Plastics required a compact thermoforming machine that could fit in a limited factory space while maintaining precision and repeatability. The challenge was to deliver a machine that met these needs without compromising on performance.

## The Solution

Machinecraft delivered the e-UNO-8060, a fully electric, zone-controlled thermoformer designed for small footprints. This machine is compact enough to fit in a single 20ft container, making it ideal for global logistics and small operations.

## Specs That Matter

- Forming area: 800 x 600 mm — perfect for versatile applications.
- Max depth: 400 mm — accommodates a range of product designs.
- Heater load: 22.4 kW total — efficient energy usage for high performance.
- Vacuum capacity: 40 m³/hr — ensures strong forming capabilities.
- Compact footprint: 1.9 x 2.7 x 3.2 m — fits in small workshops without hassle.

## The Outcome

The e-UNO-8060 has enabled Donite Plastics to expand their production capabilities with a compact solution that meets their specific needs. The machine's performance has led to increased efficiency and reduced operational costs, strengthening their market position.
