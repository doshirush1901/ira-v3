# Mirsant — High-Volume Bathtub Manufacturing Line

**Industry:** Sanitary ware | **Country:** Russia | **Year:** 2022
**Machine:** PF1-2116 | **Material:** Acrylic/ABS

---

## The Challenge

Mirsant required a high-throughput bathtub manufacturing line capable of producing 25 bathtubs per hour. The challenge lay in the demanding nature of bathtub thermoforming, which involves deep draw (860mm) and thick sheets while ensuring a flawless surface finish with no visible defects.

## The Solution

Machinecraft delivered the PF1-2116, a powerhouse capable of producing 25 bathtubs per hour. This machine features IR halogen heaters for rapid and uniform heating, temperature-controlled universal frames to prevent edge defects, and an autoloader for continuous operation.

## Specs That Matter

- Forming area: 2100 x 1600 mm — spacious enough for full-size bathtubs.
- Max depth: 860 mm — one of the deepest draws in our range, perfect for large sanitary ware.
- Heater load: 288 kW total — ensures the fastest heating for thick acrylic/ABS sheets.
- Vacuum capacity: 200 m³/hr — provides strong and consistent vacuum for quality forming.
- Production rate: 25 bathtubs/hour — industrial-scale output that meets high demand.

## The Outcome

The PF1-2116 has successfully ramped up Mirsant's production capabilities, allowing them to meet market demands efficiently. This project has strengthened Machinecraft's presence in the Russian sanitary ware market, paving the way for future collaborations.
