## The Challenge

Pinnacle Industries required a thermoforming machine capable of handling large sheets measuring 5000 x 2800 mm with a heating load of 602 kW. The challenge was to ensure uniform heating and efficient production for high-demand automotive applications.

## The Solution

Machinecraft delivered the PF1-5028 XL, a custom-built thermoforming machine designed for large formats. It features advanced heating technology and an autoloader, ensuring rapid production cycles and precision in forming.

## Specs That Matter

- Forming area: 5000 x 2800 mm — perfect for large automotive components.
- Heating load: 602 kW — ensures rapid and uniform heating for thick materials.
- Vacuum capacity: 200 m³/hr — optimal for deep draw applications.
- Autoloader included — automates sheet loading for continuous production.
- Cycle time: ~90 seconds — maximizes throughput for high-demand environments.

The PF1-5028 XL has significantly increased production capacity for Pinnacle Industries, enabling them to meet the demands of the automotive sector efficiently. The machine's performance has strengthened the partnership, paving the way for future projects.
