# PSG Signs — Polycarbonate Automotive Signage Production

**Industry:** Signage | **Country:** Canada | **Year:** 2020
**Machine:** PF1-4825, PF1-2015 | **Material:** Polycarbonate

---

## The Challenge

PSG required two thermoforming machines capable of producing large polycarbonate signage panels for automotive dealerships. The challenge was that polycarbonate is difficult to thermoform, requiring precise heating and control to avoid defects like stress whitening and optical distortion, especially when handling preprinted sheets.

## The Solution

Machinecraft delivered two fully electric, UL/CSA compliant machines designed specifically for the challenges of polycarbonate. The PF1-4825 and PF1-2015 feature advanced heating technology and a closed chamber design to ensure uniform heating without damaging preprinted graphics.

## Specs That Matter

- Forming Area: 4800 x 2500 mm (PF1-4825) — massive capacity for large signage.
- Forming Area: 2000 x 1500 mm (PF1-2015) — versatility for smaller sign production.
- Fully Electric Operation — clean, precise, and no hydraulics for better control.
- Closed Chamber Design — ensures uniform heating and prevents optical defects.
- UL/CSA Compliance — meets North American safety standards, essential for market entry.

## The Outcome

The installation of these machines has enabled PSG to produce high-quality polycarbonate signage efficiently, significantly increasing their production capabilities. This partnership has solidified Machinecraft's presence in the North American market, paving the way for future collaborations.
