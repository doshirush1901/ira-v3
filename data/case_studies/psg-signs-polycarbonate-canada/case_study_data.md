## Project Overview
PSG needed two thermoforming machines for producing large polycarbonate signage panels
for automotive dealerships across North America. The challenge: polycarbonate is notoriously
difficult to thermoform — it requires precise, uniform heating and careful process control
to avoid stress whitening, bubbling, or optical distortion. PSG needed machines that could
handle preprinted polycarbonate sheets without damaging the graphics.

## Machines
- **Machine 1:** PF1-4825 — Forming Area: 4800 x 2500 mm (massive, for large signage)
- **Machine 2:** PF1-2015 — Forming Area: 2000 x 1500 mm (for smaller signs)
- **Combined Value:** USD 750,000
