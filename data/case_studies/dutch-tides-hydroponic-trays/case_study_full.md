# Dutch Tides — Largest Thermoformer in Europe for Hydroponic Trays

## The Challenge

Dutch Tides required the largest thermoforming machine in Europe to produce massive hydroponic ebb-flow trays, measuring 6500 x 2000 mm. The challenge was to ensure high precision and efficiency in producing trays that support commercial greenhouse operations.

## The Solution

Machinecraft delivered the PF1-X-6520, a custom XL thermoformer with a 6.5 x 2 meter forming area. This full turnkey solution included machine installation and material supply, along with two weeks of on-site process optimization to ensure seamless operation.

## Specs That Matter

- Forming area: 6500 x 2000 mm — the largest thermoformer in Europe, enabling production of large hydroponic trays.
- Cycle time: ~150 seconds per cycle — efficient production for high-demand agricultural applications.
- Material compatibility: 4mm PS (Polystyrene) — ideal for hydroponic applications, ensuring durability and performance.
- Full turnkey solution: machine + material supply + on-site optimization — comprehensive support for smooth operations.
- Sustainability angle: hydroponics uses 90% less water than traditional farming — contributing to eco-friendly agricultural practices.

## The Outcome

The PF1-X-6520 is now operational at Dutch Tides, enabling them to produce high-quality hydroponic trays efficiently. This installation marks a significant achievement for Machinecraft, showcasing Indian engineering excellence in Europe.
