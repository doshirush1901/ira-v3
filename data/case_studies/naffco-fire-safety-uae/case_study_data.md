# NAFFCO FZCO — Fire Safety Equipment, UAE

## Customer
- **Name:** NAFFCO FZCO (National Fire Fighting Manufacturing FZCO)
- **Location:** Jebel Ali Free Zone, Dubai, UAE
- **Industry:** Fire safety / Emergency equipment
- **Profile:** NAFFCO is one of the largest fire safety companies in the world,
  manufacturing fire fighting vehicles, fire alarm systems, fire suppression systems,
  and safety equipment. Headquartered in Dubai with global operations.
- **Contact:** Emaad Kasab / Emad Kassab

## Project Overview
NAFFCO needed a large-format thermoforming machine to produce components for their
fire safety equipment line. The PF1-4525 with its massive 4500 x 2500 mm forming area
is one of the largest thermoforming machines in the Middle East.

## Machine
- **Model:** PF1-4525
- **Forming Area:** 4500 x 2500 mm
- **Max Depth:** 500 mm
- **Machine Number:** 21018
- **Installation Date:** 5 December 2022
- **Order Value:** USD 200,000
- **Year:** 2021 (ordered)

## Technical Specifications
- **Total Power:** 500 kW
- **Heater Type:** IR Ceramic Elstein Germany
- **Heater Elements:** 476 top + 476 bottom (952 total)
- **Vacuum:** 400 m³/hr
- **All Movements:** Electric Servo
- **Warranty:** 24 months
