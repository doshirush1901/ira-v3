# NAFFCO FZCO — Large-Format Thermoforming Machine for Fire Safety Equipment

## The Challenge

NAFFCO required a large-format thermoforming machine to produce components for their fire safety equipment line. The challenge was to find a machine capable of handling a massive 4500 x 2500 mm forming area while ensuring high precision and efficiency.

## The Solution

Machinecraft delivered the PF1-4525, one of the largest thermoforming machines in the Middle East, featuring 952 heater elements and 500 kW of power. This machine was specifically designed to meet the rigorous demands of the fire safety industry.

## Specs That Matter

- Forming Area: 4500 x 2500 mm — capable of producing large components efficiently.
- Total Power: 500 kW — ensures rapid heating and consistent performance.
- Heater Elements: 952 total (476 top + 476 bottom) — provides massive heating capacity for uniform temperature distribution.
- Vacuum: 400 m³/hr — enables quick and effective forming of complex shapes.
- Electric Servo Movements — enhances precision and reduces maintenance needs.

## The Outcome

The PF1-4525 has significantly increased NAFFCO's production capacity and efficiency, leading to a strong partnership with Machinecraft. NAFFCO has since placed additional orders, reinforcing their trust in our technology.
