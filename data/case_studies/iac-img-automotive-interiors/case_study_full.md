# IAC International Automotive India — First Made-in-India IMG Thermoforming Machine for Automotive Interiors

**Industry:** automotive | **Country:** India | **Year:** 2024
**Machine:** MC-IMG-1608 | **Material:** TPO (thermoplastic olefin)

---

## The Challenge

IAC required a locally manufactured IMG thermoforming machine to produce soft-touch, textured interior panels for vehicles. Previously, these machines were only available from European manufacturers at significantly higher costs, making it essential to develop a competitive solution that met high-quality standards.

## The Solution

Machinecraft designed and built India's first IMG thermoforming machine, the MC-IMG-1608, featuring advanced technology and local manufacturing. This machine provides the same quality and precision as European alternatives but at a fraction of the cost, supporting India's push for self-reliance in automotive manufacturing.

## Specs That Matter

- Platen Size: 1650 x 800 mm — accommodates large automotive parts.
- Cycle Time: ~120 seconds per finished part — maximizes production efficiency.
- Clamping Force: 6 tonnes (upgraded to 10 tonnes) — ensures secure forming of materials.
- Connected Load: 100 kW — powerful enough for demanding production needs.
- Siemens S7-1500 PLC + SEW servo motors — international-grade components ensure reliability.

## The Outcome

The MC-IMG-1608 has successfully entered production, enabling IAC to manufacture high-quality automotive interior components locally. This project has strengthened Machinecraft's relationship with IAC and positioned them as a key player in the Indian automotive market.
