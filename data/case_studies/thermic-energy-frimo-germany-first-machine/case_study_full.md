# Thermic Energy (via FRIMO partnership) — First Machinecraft Machine in Germany

**Industry:** Material handling / Industrial packaging | **Country:** Germany | **Year:** 2024
**Machine:** PF1-1616/S/A | **Material:** PS (Polystyrene)

---

## The Challenge

Thermic Energy required a high-performance thermoforming machine to produce material handling trays in a competitive market dominated by European manufacturers. The challenge was to deliver a machine that could meet rigorous German engineering standards while providing automation and efficiency.

## The Solution

Machinecraft delivered the PF1-1616/S/A, featuring German-sourced IR Quartz heaters and a water-cooled base for rapid cycle times. This machine is fully automated with an autoloader and servo-driven movements, showcasing Machinecraft's engineering prowess on German soil.

## Specs That Matter

- Forming area: 1600 x 1600 mm — perfect for versatile tray sizes.
- Heater load: 81 kW — ensures rapid heating for efficient production.
- Vacuum capacity: 100 m³/hr — optimal for consistent forming quality.
- Cycle time: ~90 seconds — competitive with leading European machines.
- Dual-side vacuum capability — enhances forming versatility.

## The Outcome

This installation marks a significant milestone for Machinecraft as it establishes a foothold in Germany, validating its engineering quality. The machine is fully operational, contributing to Thermic Energy's production efficiency and expanding Machinecraft's reputation in the European market.
