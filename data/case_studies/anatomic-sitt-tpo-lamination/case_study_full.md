## The Challenge

Anatomic SITT required a specialized vacuum lamination machine to bond TPO and TPU films onto complex substrates. The challenge was to achieve a seamless, high-quality finish suitable for ergonomic seating and medical applications, which demanded precision and reliability.

## The Solution

Machinecraft delivered the PF1-0810/S, a compact vacuum lamination machine featuring premium Ceramicx Ireland heaters and CE certification. This machine was specifically designed for the unique requirements of TPO and TPU materials, ensuring optimal bonding and surface quality.

## Specs That Matter

- Forming area: 800 x 1000 mm — perfect for ergonomic components.
- Max depth: 400 mm — accommodates complex shapes without compromising quality.
- Heater load: 32 kW — rapid heating for efficient production cycles.
- Vacuum capacity: 100 m³/hr — ensures strong adhesion and seamless finishes.
- CE certified — meets stringent European safety and quality standards.
