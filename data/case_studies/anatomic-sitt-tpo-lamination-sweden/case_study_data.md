# Anatomic SITT AB — TPO/TPU Vacuum Lamination, Sweden

## Customer
- **Name:** Anatomic SITT AB
- **Location:** Sweden
- **Industry:** Ergonomic seating / Medical devices / Lamination

## Project Overview
Anatomic SITT needed a specialized vacuum lamination machine for bonding TPO
(Thermoplastic Olefin) and TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) films onto substrates.
This is not traditional thermoforming — it's vacuum lamination, where a heated film
is draped and vacuum-pressed onto a 3D substrate to create a seamless, bonded surface.
Applications include ergonomic seating, medical device housings, and automotive trim.
