# Thermic Energy / FRIMO — First Machine in Germany

## Customer
- **Name:** Thermic Energy (via FRIMO partnership)
- **Location:** Germany
- **Industry:** Material handling / Industrial packaging

## Project Overview
February 2024 was a landmark month for Machinecraft — the company installed and
commissioned its first machine on German soil. Germany is the heartland of European
manufacturing and thermoforming technology, home to competitors like FRIMO, Kiefel,
and Illig. For an Indian manufacturer to place a machine in Germany is a statement
of engineering credibility.

## Machine
- **Model:** PF1-1616/S/A
- **Forming Area:** 1600 x 1600 mm (also referenced as 1300 x 1800 mm on website)
- **Max Depth:** 300 mm
- **Year:** 2022 (ordered), installed Feb 2024
- **Order Value:** EUR 189,000

## Technical Specifications
- **Heater Load:** 81 kW total
- **Heater Type:** IR Quartz TQS Germany (108 elements)
- **Vacuum:** 100 m³/hr
- **Autoloader:** Yes
- **Universal Frames:** Yes (servo-adjustable for different sheet sizes)
- **Water-Cooled Base:** Yes (for faster cycle times)
- **Cycle Time:** ~90 seconds
- **All Movements:** Servo motor driven
- **Central Cooling:** Yes
- **Special:** Vacuum on upper table for dual-side vacuum forming

## Application
- **Primary:** Material handling trays in PS (Polystyrene)
- **Materials:** ABS, ABS-TPU, HDPE, PP
- **Process:** Sheet-fed heavy gauge thermoforming with automation

## Story Angle
An Indian thermoforming machine manufacturer placing a machine in Germany — the
global capital of plastics machinery. This validates Machinecraft's engineering quality
at the highest level. The machine features German-sourced heater elements (TQS),
water-cooled base for fast cycles, and full automation.

## Key Proof Points
- First Machinecraft machine installed in Germany
- Fully automated: autoloader + universal frames + servo-driven
- German-quality components: TQS heaters, water-cooled base
- 90-second cycle time (competitive with European machines)
- Dual-side vacuum capability (upper table vacuum)
- Competitive price point vs. European alternatives
