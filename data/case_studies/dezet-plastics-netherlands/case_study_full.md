# Dezet Plastics — Legacy Machine Replacement for Enhanced Throughput

**Industry:** Precision thermoforming | **Country:** Netherlands | **Year:** 2025
**Machine:** PF1-X-1210 | **Material:** Various thermoplastics

---

## The Challenge

Dezet Plastics needed to replace a 42-year-old German Illig thermoforming machine that was the backbone of their production. The challenge was to find a machine that could match the reliability and precision of the Illig while enhancing throughput and modernizing their operations.

## The Solution

Machinecraft provided the PF1-X-1210, a fully servo-driven thermoforming machine with dual-sided IR quartz heating and Mitsubishi PLC + HMI controls. This machine not only matched the precision of the old Illig but also increased throughput by 40%, offering modern energy-efficient processing.

## Specs That Matter

- Forming Area: 1200 x 1000 mm — Ideal for precision components.
- Throughput: 20-22 sheets/hour — 40% increase over the previous machine.
- Drive System: Fully servo-driven — Ensures precise, intelligent movements.
- Heaters: Dual-sided IR quartz heating — Provides consistent, energy-efficient processing.
- Control System: Mitsubishi PLC + HMI — Offers advanced control and profiling capabilities.

## The Outcome

The PF1-X-1210 was installed in October 2025, replacing the old Illig machine. It significantly improved Dezet's production capabilities, increasing throughput by 40% and reducing maintenance issues. The project strengthened the relationship with Machinecraft, showcasing the potential for future collaborations.
