# Dezet Plastics — Replacing a 42-Year-Old German Machine in South Holland

## Customer
- **Name:** Dezet Plastics
- **Location:** South Holland, Netherlands
- **Industry:** Precision thermoforming — mobility, aerospace, medical
- **Profile:** Family-owned Dutch company, 40+ years in thermoforming. Known for Dutch principles
  of precision, reliability, and unwavering quality. Trusted partner to industries that demand excellence.
- **Products:** Specialized components for elderly mobility vehicles, precision parts for aircraft
  training simulators, durable first aid ECR kit boxes.

## Project Overview
Dezet's 1983 German-made Illig thermoforming machine had been the heartbeat of their production floor
for 42 years. Every morning, operators arrived to its familiar hum — a rhythmic pulse that had shaped
thousands of quality products and countless careers. But standing still means falling behind.

When the faithful 1983 machine retired, Dezet honored its legacy while embracing the future with
the Machinecraft PF1-X-1210. This is their philosophy: quality over quantity, relationships over
transactions, and progress that never forgets where it came from.

## Machine
- **Model:** PF1-X-1210
- **Forming Area:** 1200 x 1000 mm
- **Investment:** EUR 150,000
- **Year:** 2025 (installed October 2025)
- **Replaced:** 1983 German-made Illig thermoforming machine (42 years of service)

## Results — Measured Impact
- **Throughput:** Jumped from 14-16 sheets/hour to 20-22 sheets/hour — a **40% increase**
- **Fully servo-driven system** replaced mechanical components, bringing intelligence to every movement
- **Dual-sided IR quartz heating** ensured consistent, energy-efficient processing
- **Mitsubishi PLC + HMI control** gave operators unprecedented control and profiling capabilities
- **Closed chamber design** with pre-blow and vacuum profiling improved precision and sheet utilization

## Technical Specifications
- **Drive System:** Fully servo-driven (all movements)
- **Heaters:** Dual-sided IR quartz heating (top and bottom)
- **PLC:** Mitsubishi
- **HMI:** Mitsubishi touchscreen with recipe management
- **Chamber:** Closed chamber design
- **Features:** Pre-blow capability, vacuum profiling
- **Design:** Sleek, compact
