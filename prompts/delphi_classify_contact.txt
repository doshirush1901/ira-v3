Your task: classify a contact into exactly ONE of these categories, or reject them.

VALID CRM CATEGORIES (include in CRM):
- LIVE_CUSTOMER: Currently has an active order, ongoing machine build, or recent delivery (within last 2 years) with active support/communication.
- PAST_CUSTOMER: Purchased a machine in the past but no active orders. May have gone quiet or the project is complete.
- LEAD_WITH_INTERACTIONS: Has NOT purchased yet, but there is evidence of engagement — quotes sent, meetings held, emails exchanged, exhibition meetings, phone calls, or any two-way communication.
- LEAD_NO_INTERACTIONS: Appears in a contact list, exhibition visitor list, or database but there is NO evidence of any two-way communication or engagement. Cold contact only.

REJECT CATEGORIES (do NOT include in CRM):
- VENDOR: Supplies materials, components, or services TO Machinecraft. Examples:
  iM Technologies (CAD design services), apra-plast (product management).
  Key signals: invoices FROM them to Machinecraft, they appear in vendor payment
  records, they provide services/parts rather than buying machines.
- PARTNER: Channel partner, reseller, agent, or collaborator. Examples: FRIMO
  (co-exhibitor), Mikhail Zraychenko (Russia/CIS sales agent, mvzet61@mail.ru),
  Gerwin Pelle (Netherlands agent, gerwinpelle@cncteam.nl).
  Key signals: they send inquiries "on behalf of" someone else, they represent
  Machinecraft in a region, they are cc'd on customer emails but are not the buyer,
  they negotiate pricing for a third party, they discuss commission or agency terms.
- OWN_COMPANY: Machinecraft employee or internal contact (@machinecraft.org,
  @machinecraft.in). Also includes Formpack (@formpack.in, sister company).
- OTHER: Recruiters, spam, personal contacts, unrelated to Machinecraft business.

CLASSIFICATION SIGNALS:
- Order/PO history → LIVE_CUSTOMER or PAST_CUSTOMER
- Active machine build or recent delivery → LIVE_CUSTOMER
- Quotes sent but no order → LEAD_WITH_INTERACTIONS
- Meeting notes, exhibition meetings, email exchanges → LEAD_WITH_INTERACTIONS
- Only appears in a bulk list with no interaction evidence → LEAD_NO_INTERACTIONS
- @machinecraft.org or @machinecraft.in email → OWN_COMPANY
- Supplies raw materials, components, shipping → VENDOR
- Reseller, agent, channel partner → PARTNER
