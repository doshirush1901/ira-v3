Why This Was Not Possible Two Years Ago

Three things converged in 2025-2026:

  1. Tool-calling LLMs. GPT-4o can decide which tool to use, call it, read the result, and decide what to do next — in a loop. This lets Plutus say "I need vendor data," call Hera, then say "I need the payment schedule," read the PDF, then combine both. Before 2024, every workflow had to be hardcoded.
  2. Cheap, fast embeddings. Voyage AI gives us 1024-dimension vectors at scale. 636+ documents indexed — quotes, POs, manuals, emails, contracts. Any agent can search any document in milliseconds. Two years ago: 10x cost, 10x slower.
  3. Memory-as-a-service. Mem0 gives each agent persistent memory. When Nemesis learns a correction, that fact is available to every agent forever. The system learns from its mistakes. This product did not exist before 2025.

We built this in 4-5 months. In 2023, this would have taken a team of 10 engineers two years.

The FICCI CFO Summit 2026 (happening this month) is literally themed "Reimagining the Finance Role in the Age of AI." We are not imagining it. We built it.
