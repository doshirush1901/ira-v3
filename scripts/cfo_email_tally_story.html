Chapter 2: What We Owe

But here is the part that made me pause. Of those 632 bills:

628 are overdue.

That is 99.4%.

The oldest bill is 895 days old. That is almost two and a half years. That vendor — Pinnacle Industries — has been waiting since September 2023. They have seen two Diwalis come and go while waiting for their Rs. 11.8 Lakhs.

Now, most of these are not crisis-level individually. Many are small amounts from local vendors who know us and are patient. But the pattern is not great. When nearly every single bill is overdue, it stops being "a few delayed payments" and starts being "how we operate." And that is a reputation risk.
